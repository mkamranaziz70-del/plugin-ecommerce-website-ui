Newcastle's Premier Edible Printing Studio

Founded in the heart of Newcastle, Newy Edible Toppers began with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between digital design and artisanal baking. We recognized that every celebration deserves a centerpiece that is as unique as the story behind it.

Today, we utilize state-of-the-art high-definition printing technology and premium vegan-friendly icing sheets to deliver professional-grade results to bakeries, corporate partners, and home decorators across Australia.

Premium Quality

We use only the finest edible inks and premium icing sheets to ensure vibrant colors and sharp details on every print.

Fast Turnaround

Based in Newcastle, we offer rapid shipping Australia-wide, ensuring your toppers arrive fresh and ready for your event.

Bespoke Service

From corporate logos to personal photos, our design studio allows you to create truly one-of-a-kind edible masterpieces.
