Design Guidelines

Our Round Birthday Cake Toppers are printed on premium edible wafer paper using food-safe, vibrant inks. Each topper is personalised with your choice of name, age, and message — making every celebration truly special.

Perfect for standard round cakes, they peel easily from the backing and apply directly onto icing, fondant, or buttercream. No special skills required — just peel, place, and celebrate!

  • Printed with premium, food-safe edible inks
  • Available in 6cm, 8cm, and 10cm diameters
  • Suitable for all icing types: fondant, buttercream, ganache
  • Despatched same day if ordered before 12pm AEST
  • Individually wrapped in food-safe packaging
  • Made fresh in Newcastle, NSW — not overseas
  • Shelf-stable for up to 6 months when stored correctly
  • Gluten-free & nut-free (wafer paper option)
