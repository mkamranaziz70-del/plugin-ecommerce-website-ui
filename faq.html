Ordering & Pricing

How do I place an order?
Simply browse our shop and pick the design you love, or use our custom order form to upload your own image, logo or text. Choose your size, sheet type and quantity, fill in your details, and we'll send a free artwork preview before printing. Most orders ship the same day if placed before 12pm.
How much do edible toppers cost?
Pricing starts from $6.99 for a 6cm round topper and goes up based on size and finish. Custom designs from your photo or logo are $8.99 for a standard 8cm round. Bulk pricing is available for orders of 10+ — just contact us.
Do you offer bulk or wholesale pricing?
Yes! We work with bakeries, event planners and corporate clients. Discounts kick in at 10+ toppers and we offer a dedicated wholesale program for regular orders. Get in touch for a quote.
Can I cancel or change my order after placing it?
If we haven't started printing yet, absolutely — just email hello@newyedibletoppers.com.au with your order number. Once printing has started we may not be able to make changes, so double-check your artwork preview!
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards — Visa, Mastercard and AMEX — securely processed via Stripe. All payments are encrypted end-to-end with 256-bit SSL.

Custom Designs

What image formats do you accept?
We accept JPG, PNG and PDF. For the best print quality, please upload at 300 DPI minimum. PNG with a transparent background is ideal for logos.
Can you use a photo from my phone?
Absolutely — modern phone photos are usually high enough quality. Make sure the subject is in focus, well-lit and not cropped tightly. Our team will check the resolution before printing and email you if anything needs adjusting.
Do you do edits or design work?
Yes! Light edits like adding a name, changing colours or removing a background are included free with every custom order. More involved design work (creating from scratch, redrawing logos) is $15–$45 depending on complexity.
Are there any image restrictions?
We can't print copyrighted character art (Disney, Marvel etc.) unless you can show licensed permission. Otherwise, we have plenty of original character-style designs available in our shop.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take?
  • Standard (Australia-wide): 3–5 business days · $6.95
  • Express Post: 1–2 business days · $12.95
  • Newcastle Local Pickup: Free · ready same day
Shipping is free on orders over $50.
Do you ship internationally?
Currently we ship within Australia only — edible products have strict customs rules overseas. We're working on expanding to NZ in 2026!
When will my order be dispatched?
Pre-designed toppers placed before 12pm Mon–Fri ship the same day. Custom orders ship within 24 hours of you approving the artwork preview. You'll get tracking the moment we hand it to the courier.

Using Your Topper

How do I apply the topper to my cake?
Peel the topper away from the backing sheet just before applying. Place gently onto buttercream, fondant, ganache or whipped cream — the moisture activates a natural bond. Avoid placing on dry surfaces like dry sponge or chocolate (the topper needs moisture to stick).
How long do toppers last?
Sealed in their original packaging, our toppers stay fresh for 6–9 months. Once applied to the cake, the cake should be eaten within 2–3 days as normal.
How should I store my topper before use?
Store in the original sealed bag at room temperature, away from direct sunlight, heat and moisture. Do not refrigerate — condensation will warp the icing sheet.
Are toppers allergy-friendly?
Our standard icing is gluten-free, nut-free, dairy-free and vegetarian. We also offer a fully vegan wafer option. Always check the ingredients list on your packaging if you have specific allergies.

Returns & Refunds

What's your refund policy?
Because each topper is custom-made and edible, we don't accept change-of-mind returns. However, if your order arrives damaged, mis-printed, or different to what you approved, we'll reprint and ship a replacement at no cost — just send a photo within 48 hours.
My topper arrived damaged — what now?
Email hello@newyedibletoppers.com.au with your order number and a photo of the damaged item within 48 hours of delivery. We'll express-ship a replacement that same day, no questions asked.
