Our Story

Made with Love in Newy

A small Newcastle business with a big love for celebration — turning your favourite memories into edible art since 2021.

Newcastle, NSW
Hello, we're Newy!

Bringing your sweetest moments to life, one topper at a time.

Newy Edible Toppers started as a kitchen-table side project in Newcastle. We saw friends struggling to find quality edible prints that actually tasted good and looked vibrant on the cake.

Today we're a passionate small team using high-definition printing technology and premium icing sheets to deliver professional-grade results to home bakers, professional cake decorators and corporate clients across Australia.

Every topper is printed fresh, packaged carefully and shipped quickly — because your celebration deserves nothing less.
